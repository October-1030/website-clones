# Storybound Page Topology

1. Sticky navigation — 64px, translucent dark green, brand, anchor links, docs link, download CTA.
2. Hero — announcement pill, serif headline, supporting copy, Windows/Mac CTAs, proof row, detailed product-window mockup.
3. How it works — three numbered cards explaining paste, wait, and open in Jianying.
4. Features — four alternating two-column cards: rewriting, image generation/gallery, voice library, Jianying draft export.
5. Tracks — eight compact content-track cards and a strategy note.
6. Pipeline — seven numbered production steps plus editable/retry tags.
7. Pricing — monthly, annual, lifetime cards, credit recharge packs, and purchase CTAs.
8. Proof — real metrics, group/private-chat evidence, and four comment screenshots.
9. FAQ — nine native disclosure rows.
10. Final CTA — download and demo actions with atmospheric glow/sparks.
11. Footer — QR code, product/support links, copyright and policy links.
12. Fixed back-to-top control — appears after 600px.

The page uses normal document flow with a sticky navigation and one fixed decorative grain overlay. There is no carousel, tab switcher, scroll snap, or time-driven content rotation.
